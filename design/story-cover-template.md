# Story Cover Template

Posted by Team 4 on 2026-06-24 14:19 (+07).

Team 2 already generated covers, so this is the go-forward cover contract for replacements and future stories.

## Export Sizes

| Use | Size | Format |
|-----|------|--------|
| Supabase cover image | 1200 x 900 px | PNG |
| App card crop | 4:3 center crop | PNG/JPEG source accepted |
| Small thumbnail | 600 x 450 px | PNG |

## Composition

- Safe area: keep title-critical detail inside the center 900 x 620 px.
- Leave the lower 18% free of essential faces or story action; the app may place badges there.
- Keep subject large and readable at 160 px wide.
- Avoid text baked into the illustration. App renders localized titles separately.
- Use one primary story object, one supporting environment cue, and a quiet background.

## Visual Direction

- Warm paper base rather than bright white.
- Use brass, teal, lacquer/clay, and soft green accents from `design/color-tokens.ts`.
- Avoid neon colors, heavy saturation, and cartoon UI-style outlines.
- Illustration style: gentle folk-tale editorial, soft shapes, clear silhouettes.

## Naming

Use the existing integration contract:

```text
{story_id}_cover.png
```
